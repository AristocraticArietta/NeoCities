## About

This is my first in a series of analog photo dumps that I hope to do often. I want to be taking more photos so what better way to encourage myself than to start a blog series? This first dump includes pictures that have taken over the last several years, in no particular order. Many of the pictures are of my dog, but he's a cutie so I'm sure you won't mind. :) Some of the scans aren't the best, but many of the pictures are from before I started scanning my own photos. Some day, I will re-scan them. But that day is not today. Anyways, here it is!
