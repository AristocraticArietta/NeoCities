## About this series
I have found that I go through phases when it comes to my YouTube addiction. Sometimes it's anime AMVs, movie trailers for movies that I probably won't watch, or some random rabbit hole that I go down and then suddenly I know way too much about something. This is a series of those rabbit holes. I hope to keep this series going every month, so I can chronicle my YouTube habits and share some cool stuff with you all. I will update this list throughout the month, so check back and see how it evolves!

## My Favorite Videos - July 2026

Here are some of my favorite videos I've watched recently. I've been watching a lot more YouTube during the summer, as I don't have much to do right now. I have never played the game Ready or Not, and I strongly suspect it to be copaganda, but the lore seems pretty interesting and this editor did a great job of telling a story. It actually seems like this game attempts to be nuanced about the role of police in society, but who knows how well it is executed. This GMV definitely takes a stand. It says that police are the ones on the front lines of a system broken by those much more powerful than them, i.e. politicians. The policing system in America is broken as well, with many corrupt and immoral police officers who are trained to strike first and ask questions later. Criminals are often just disadvantaged people who have been repeatedly failed by this broken system and have decided to take matters into their own hands, which makes them sympathetic characters. I definitely want to write a blog post about how the policing system in America is broken, as this blub is getting pretty long already and I have much more to say. In the end, the GMV is pretty cool and I like it for its nuance. Also, I love this song so much, and it's a great choice for an GMV like this.
</FavoriteVideo>
